About us

GreenPulse

GreenPulse is a pioneering clean energy startup dedicated to transforming rural landscapes through affordable, accessible solar technology. With a deep commitment to environmental stewardship and social equity, we design and deploy smart solar systems tailored to underserved communities, empowering them with the tools for self-reliance and growth.

Mission

To democratize access to clean energy by providing innovative, reliable, and affordable solar solutions to rural and remote regions. GreenPulse aims to reduce carbon footprints while boosting local economies through sustainable power systems and community-centered engagement.

Vision

To be a global leader in renewable energy innovationâ€”creating a future where every household, regardless of geography or income, has access to clean electricity. GreenPulse envisions a world energized by sustainability, guided by innovation, and driven by inclusivity.

History

Founded in 2021 by a group of eco-conscious engineers and social entrepreneurs, GreenPulse began as a garage project in Pune, India. Frustrated by the unreliable grid in rural Maharashtra, the founders designed a modular solar panel system that could scale with household needs.
